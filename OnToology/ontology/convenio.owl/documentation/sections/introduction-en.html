Introduction back to ToC

This vocabulary represents the data on agreements, that is to say agreements with legal effects adopted by municipalities with other public administrations, public bodies, public law entities, other municipalities, public universities, or with private entities for a common purpose. This vocabulary has been developed in the context of the project "Plataforma de gobierno abierto, colaborativa e interoperable" with the participation of the city councils of Madrid, Zaragoza, Santiago de Compostela and A Coru√±a. The vocabulary refers to terms of other models for the representation of concepts in domains such as organizations, territorial administrative divisions and digital documents. Additionally it is related to the vocabulary of [subventions](http://vocab.linkeddata.es/datosabiertos/def/sector-publico/subvencion/index-en.html) given that an agreement can implement a subvention. Specifically, for the representation of documents associated to an agreement such as the text of the agreement and other related documents, we reuse the ontology [schema.org](http://schema.org/). The [organizations](https://www.w3.org/TR/vocab-org/) vocabulary is reused to represent the government area that is in charge of the agreement, the entities that subscribe an agreement, the position of the person that is the agreement signee for the entity as well as the position of the person that is the agreement signee for the city council. The [territory](http://vocab.linkeddata.es/datosabiertos/def/sector-publico/territorio#) vocabulary is reused which contains the administrative divisions that represent the district that may be in charge of the agreement or the scope of an administrative unit, for example a municipality in general or a specific district.
