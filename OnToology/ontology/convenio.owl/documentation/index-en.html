Vocabulary for the representation of data on agreements adopted by municipalities with other entities for a common purpose.

This version:
http://vocab.ciudadesabiertas.es/def/sector-publico/convenio/1.0.0
Revision:
This is the initial version of the ontology of Agreements that has been implemented in the context of the project "Plataforma de gobierno abierto, colaborativa e interoperable" with the participation of the city councils of Madrid, Zaragoza, Santiago de Composteka, and A Coruña.
